Real-World Applications of Calculus

1. Engineering – Suspension Bridge Design

Calculating the bending moment for a uniformly distributed load:

\[ M(x) = \frac{w}{2}x(L - x) \]

Used to determine structural load and stress points.

2. Physics – Spacecraft Landing on Mars

Using integration to calculate velocity and position under Martian gravity:

\[ v(t) = -3.71t + v_0 \]

\[ s(t) = -1.855t^2 + v_0t + s_0 \]

3. Economics – Profit Maximization

Finding the maximum profit point by setting the derivative of profit to zero:

\[ P(x) = -2x^2 + 100x - 500 \]

\[ P'(x) = -4x + 100 \Rightarrow x = 25 \]

4. Environmental Science – Rainwater Runoff

Total rainwater accumulation over 2 hours from a linear rate:

\[ r(t) = 5t \]

\[ \int_0^2 5t \, dt = 10 \text{ liters} \]

5. Computer Science – Simulating Object Movement

Physics simulation under gravity:

\[ a(t) = -9.8 \Rightarrow v(t) = -9.8t + v_0 \]

\[ s(t) = -4.9t^2 + v_0t + s_0 \]

6. Biology/Medicine – Drug Absorption

Modeling drug concentration over time with exponential decay:

\[ C(t) = C_0 e^{-kt} \]

\[ \text{AUC} = \int_0^\infty C_0 e^{-kt} dt = \frac{C_0}{k} \]
